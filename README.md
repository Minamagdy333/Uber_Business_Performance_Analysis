# Data Analysis-Uber-App
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze Uber ride-booking data. The project focuses on revenue analysis, booking performance, customer behavior, and payment trends, enabling stakeholders to identify growth opportunities, improve operational efficiency, and support strategic decision-making.
# Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze Uber ride-booking data to understand revenue performance, booking trends, customer behavior, vehicle-type performance, and payment preferences. The analysis aims to identify the most profitable vehicle categories, evaluate booking completion rates, measure customer demand, and uncover business opportunities for improving revenue and operational efficiency.
# Business Questions
Revenue Analysis \
1-What is the total revenue generated by the business?\
2-Which vehicle type generates the highest revenue?\
3-Which vehicle type generates the lowest revenue?\
4-How does revenue vary across months for each vehicle type?\
5-Which month records the highest revenue performance?\
